Vision
To be a globally admired university known for its academic excellence, innovative research, and holistic development of students.
Mission
* Provide ICT enabled teaching and learning environment.
* Enable students to become digital age literates and professionals.
* Provide knowledge and skills to succeed as engineers and enterpreneurs.
* Prepare graduates for lifelong and ethical challenges.
Program Specific Outcomes(PSOs)
* PSO1 : Demonstrate skills and competence in the analysis, design and development of computer-based system addressing industrial and social issues.
* PSO2 : Tackle future technological challenges in areas like security, AR, Iot, etc.
