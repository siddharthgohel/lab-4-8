Vision

Our vision is to be a leading institution in engineering education, fostering innovation and excellence in research and development.

Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality education that empowers students to become skilled professionals and leaders in their fields.

Program specific outcomes(PSOs)

Upon completion of the program, students will be able to:

  • Demonstrate a thorough understanding of computer science fundamentals.
  • Apply engineering principles to solve complex problems.
  • Engage in lifelong learning and professional development.

Courses Offered

We offer a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in computer science and engineering, including:

  • Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science
  • Master of Technology (M.Tech) in Computer Science
  • Ph.D. in Computer Science
